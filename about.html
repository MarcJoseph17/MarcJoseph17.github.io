MARC'S JOURNEY

About Marc


What to know...


Motivated with an efficient and organized work ethic, strong analytical planning skills, combined with the ability to synchronize the efforts of many to reach organizational goals. Ability to implement various solutions for clients while taking on numerous projects. Upon graduation Marc has worked with two notable companies from which he gained immense experience and industry insight, one being Greystar where Marc was a full-time leasing agent. Here Marc was responsible for identifying new opportunities for business growth and optimizing marketing strategies, as well as analyzing business processes and finances under the district manager Theresa Barrios. Marc then found new opportunity in a role at Walter Wealth Management as their new Business Development Specialist in early 2021, here Marc was able to help grow Walser's customer base and increase managed accounts. He was also responsible for sourcing new leads, making outbound calls to potential customers, and taking inbound calls from existing clients and ensuring their continued satisfaction.

Fun facts...


Marc began his early education at St. John Vianney High School where he was a two-sport varsity athlete in soccer, and volleyball. Marc also received a notable award for 500+hrs of Christian Service. He then continued his education at Monmouth University where he received a dual concentrated degree in Marketing & Management. Upon graduation, he received an offer to intern at CMDS (https://www.cmdsonline.com) an advertising agency in Colts Neck, NJ. Here is where Marc received vast insight on key technical & operational skills while shadowing Project mgmt, the Director of Marketing, the Creative Designers and Developers, as well as Chris Mullvaney the CEO. This year long internship is what led mark to have a much bigger passion for the creative arts.
